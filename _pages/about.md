I am a master student at Shandong University, advised by [Prof. Xiankai Lu](https://faculty.sdu.edu.cn/luxiankai/zh_CN/index.htm).

My primary academic interest is zero-shot learning, out of distribution classification, human pose and shape estimation. I tend to analyze the difficult points of a problem through summarization and analysis, and solve the problem by combining theoretical methods.
